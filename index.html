Welsh Cakes

Prep time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 55g castor sugar
  • 110g butter
  • 1-2 handfuls of sultanas
  • 1-2 medium eggs

Method

  1. Sieve the flour into a large mixing bowl, add the butter and rub together using your fingertips until you get a fine crumbly texture.
  2. Add the sugar and sultanas and mix together.
  3. Add 1 egg and mix. The mixture should bind together. If the mixture is too dry add another egg.
  4. Roll the mixture out on a floured surface until the mixture is about a quarter of an inch thick.
  5. Cut out welsh cakes using a 2 inch diameter cutter.
  6. Heat a greased bake stone (or cast iron skillet) to a medium heat. Cook the welshcakes (4/5 at a time) on the bake stone for 4-5 minutes each side.
  7. Dust with castor sugar and enjoy!
